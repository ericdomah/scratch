## 🌟 The "Golden Four" (Essential for Manuscript)

### 1. The Comparison Victory (`final_roc_comparison.png`)
*   **Where to use:** The "Model Evaluation" or "Results" chapter.
*   **Why it matters:** This is your primary proof. It shows your **Context-Aware model** significantly outperforming industry baselines. It visually demonstrates the value of your "Smart Grid Context" approach.

### 2. The Classification Proof (`final_confusion_matrix.png`)
*   **Where to use:** The "Analysis of Results" section.
*   **Why it matters:** It provides the raw numbers (True Positives, False Alarms) that your committee will look for. It proves the model is reliable for real-world utility deployment.

### 3. The Forensic Transparency (`xai_report.png`)
*   **Where to use:** The "Explainability" or "Discussion" chapter.
*   **Why it matters:** It transforms your AI from a "Black Box" into a "Trustworthy System." Use this to show how field technicians can see exactly why a meter was flagged.

### 4. The Methodology Validation (`training_loss_curve.png`)
*   **Where to use:** The "Methodology" or "Model Training" chapter.
*   **Why it matters:** It proves that your model learned correctly and converged stably, ensuring your findings are scientifically reproducible.
